About AfSNET

AfSNET – Empowering Sub-Sovereign Governments in African Trade

In 2021, African Export-Import Bank (“Afreximbank” or “the Bank”) established the African Sub-Sovereign Governments Network (“AfSNET” or “the Network”) as a vehicle for promoting intra-African trade, investment and industrialisation at the Sub-Sovereign level.
