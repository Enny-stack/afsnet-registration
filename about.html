About AfSNET

AfSNET – Empowering Sub-Sovereign Governments in African Trade

In 2021, African Export-Import Bank (“Afreximbank” or “the Bank”) established the African Sub-Sovereign Governments Network (“AfSNET” or “the Network”) as a vehicle for promoting intra-African trade, investment and industrialisation at the Sub-Sovereign level.

The Bank defines an African Sub-Sovereign Government as a division of a sovereign State designated as a region, state, province, or local authority, with delineated governance and powers within which they operate. Typically, a sub-sovereign government will have its own legislature and mandate, which includes the collection of revenue, expenditure, socio-economic development, and promotion of trade and investment in the area under its jurisdiction.

The Bank therefore established the African Sub-Sovereign Governments Network to promote intra-African trade and investment, educational and cultural exchanges and effective and active engagement among sub-sovereigns in the context of the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA), for the development and prosperity of the continent. The Network, therefore, aims to create an interactive platform for Government to Government (G2G), Business to Government (B2G) and Business to Business (B2B) information exchanges and creation of synergies in a structured and deliberate manner.

Key Objectives of AfSNET

Implementing the African Direct Investment Initiative (ADI)

To provide a platform for the practical implementation of the Bank’s African Direct Investment initiative by mobilising private capital within Africa and deploying it to deserving sub-sovereigns for industrialisation and developmental needs.

Facilitating Public-Private Collaboration and Capacity Building

To provide a platform for public-private collaboration, peer learning, and capacity building, particularly for Investment Promotion Agencies (IPAs) at the sub-sovereign level.

Think Tank for Sub-Sovereign Trade & Investment

To act as a think tank and centre of excellence on trade and investment matters at the sub-sovereign level.

Regional Integration & AfCFTA

To deepen regional integration and support the implementation of the AfCFTA.

Investor Matchmaking & Local Partnerships

To facilitate matchmaking and twinning between investors, the local private sector, and project sponsors under the aegis of sub-sovereign governments.

Promoting Infrastructure & Investment

To promote infrastructure, trade, and investment in sub-sovereign regions.

Boosting Sub-Sovereign Investment Collaboration

To enhance collaboration among sub-sovereigns on investment initiatives.

Driving Adoption of Afreximbank Products

To act as a conduit to drive adoption of the Bank’s products such as ATG, Border Markets, TRADAR Club, ETCs, Buyer Programme, SME Programme, How to Export, PPF, EPC Initiative, etc.

Deals

To support bankable investment deals in African regions.
